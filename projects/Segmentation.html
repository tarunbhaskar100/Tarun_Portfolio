Semantic Segmentation: Enhancing Scene Understanding in Cityscapes

  • Time Duration: Jun 2020 - Aug 2020
  • Tech Stack: Deep Learning , Python (Programming Language) , Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) , Computer Vision , NumPy , TensorFlow , OpenCV

Implemented Semantic Segmentation on the Cityscapes dataset to enhance scene understanding by assigning semantic labels (e.g., car, people, road, etc.) to each pixel in an image based on its corresponding class.

Developed a novel model and conducted a comprehensive comparative study with existing state-of-the-art models. The project showcased my expertise in advanced computer vision techniques and deep learning algorithms for scene analysis in urban environments.
